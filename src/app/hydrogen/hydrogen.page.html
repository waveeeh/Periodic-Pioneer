Hydrogen
Nonmetal Hydrogen is a chemical element; it has symbol H and atomic number 1. It is the lightest element and, at standard conditions, is a gas of diatomic molecules with the formula H₂. It is colorless, odorless, tasteless, non-toxic, and highly combustible.
